# Inventory Reports

Understand your stock health, find slow-moving items, and optimize inventory.

---

## Stock Health Dashboard

Go to **Warehouse > Reports** for an overview of your inventory performance:

- **Turnover rate** — how fast you sell through stock (higher is better)
- **Days of inventory** — how many days your current stock will last
- **Stockout rate** — percentage of time items are out of stock
- **Fill rate** — percentage of orders you can fulfill completely
- **Dead stock** — items with no movement in 90+ days

[Go to Reports](/dashboard/warehouse/reports)

---

## Stock Aging

See how old your inventory is, grouped by age buckets:

| Bucket | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| 0-30 days | Fresh stock |
| 31-60 days | Normal |
| 61-90 days | Getting old |
| 91-180 days | Slow-moving |
| 181-365 days | Very slow |
| 365+ days | Dead stock |

Items in the **91+ day** buckets are candidates for clearance sales, returns to supplier, or write-off.

---

## Stock Turnover

For each product, see:
- **Cost of goods sold** in the period
- **Average inventory value**
- **Turnover rate** (COGS divided by average inventory)
- **Days of inventory** (how long current stock will last)

Filter by time period (7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or 1 year).

---

## Availability Matrix

See stock availability across all your branches at a glance:

- Rows = products
- Columns = branches
- Each cell shows on-hand, reserved, and available quantities

Use this to find which branch has surplus stock for transfers.

---

## Cost & Valuation

Go to **Warehouse > Cost** to see:

- **Inventory valuation** — total value of all stock using your configured method (WAC, FIFO, or FEFO)
- **Cost layers** — individual receipt-level cost entries, showing how each batch was priced

---

## Traceability

Go to **Warehouse > Trace** to track individual lots or serial numbers:

- **Trace Lot** — see everywhere a batch has been: which branches, which customers, which moves
- **Trace Serial** — full history of one specific item from receipt to current location
- **Recall Analysis** — identify all affected locations and shipments if a lot needs to be recalled

> Traceability requires **Enterprise** mode.

[Go to Traceability](/dashboard/warehouse/trace)
