# Daily Stock Management

What you do every day to keep your stock accurate.

---

## Check Stock Levels

Go to **Inventory > Stock Levels**. This shows every product with its current stock for your active branch.

- Use the **search bar** to find products by name, SKU, or barcode
- Use the **category filter** to narrow by product type
- Click a product row to see its full details in the side panel

[Go to Stock Levels](/dashboard/inventory)

---

## Handle Low Stock Alerts

Go to **Inventory > Low Stock**. This lists all products below their minimum stock threshold.

- Products show **current stock** vs **minimum threshold**
- Click any item to quickly adjust stock or create a purchase

> **Tip:** Set up **Replenishment Rules** in the Warehouse section to get automatic reorder suggestions.

[Go to Low Stock](/dashboard/inventory/low-stock)

---

## Make a Stock Adjustment

When stock doesn't match reality (damage, theft, recount):

1. Go to **Inventory > Stock Levels**
2. Find the product
3. Click **Set Stock** (or the pencil icon)
4. Enter the corrected quantity and a reason
5. Click **Save**

Every adjustment is recorded in the **Movements** page with who made it and why.

---

## View Stock Movements

Go to **Inventory > Movements** to see the full audit trail:

- Every purchase, transfer, adjustment, and sale is logged
- Filter by date, product, or movement type
- Export for external reporting

[Go to Movements](/dashboard/inventory/movements)

---

## Scan a Barcode

If your products have barcodes:

1. Go to **Warehouse > Scan** (or use the scan button on any page)
2. Scan or type the barcode
3. The system resolves it to a product, lot, serial, location, or package

The scanner works with standard retail barcodes, GS1-128 warehouse labels, and QR codes.
