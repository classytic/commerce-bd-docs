# Roles & Permissions

Who can do what in accounting. Buttons you don't have access to won't appear in the UI.

---

## Who can post, pay, and issue notes

These actions require **Admin** or **Finance Admin** role:

- Post a vendor bill
- Pay a supplier
- Post a credit note (supplier return/allowance)
- Post a customer invoice
- Record a customer receipt
- Post a debit note (customer return/allowance)
- Post an opening balance
- Edit customer credit terms (limit, days)
- Close / reopen a day

---

## Who can view reports

These are available to anyone with a finance role:

- **Admin**, **Finance Admin**, **Finance Manager**, **Branch Manager**

You can view:
- Vendor bills and customer invoices (open items)
- A/P and A/R aging reports
- Partner statements
- Trial balance, balance sheet, income statement, general ledger, cash flow
- Budget vs actual

---

## Sidebar visibility

The entire Accounting section in the sidebar is only visible to users with a finance role. If you don't see it, ask your admin to assign you the appropriate role.

---

## What if I see an error?

If you click a button and see "Permission denied":
- Your role may not have access to that specific action
- Ask your admin to check your role assignment in **User Management**

If you see a button that shouldn't be there for your role, that's a bug — report it to your system administrator.
