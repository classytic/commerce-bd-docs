# Reports & Analysis

All reports run on live data. Select a date range and click **Run Report**.

---

## A/P Aging — Who Do You Owe?

Shows all outstanding supplier balances grouped by how overdue they are:

| Bucket | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Current** | Due within 30 days |
| **31–60** | 31 to 60 days past due |
| **61–90** | 61 to 90 days past due |
| **90+** | More than 90 days past due |

Click a supplier row to drill into their full statement. Export as CSV or print.

[Open A/P Aging](/dashboard/accounting/reports/ap-aging)

---

## A/R Aging — Who Owes You?

Same layout for customer receivables. Use this to:
- Spot customers falling behind on payments
- Prioritize collection on 90+ day items
- Track how well your credit terms are working

[Open A/R Aging](/dashboard/accounting/reports/ar-aging)

## Partner Statement

Detailed history for a single supplier or customer over a period:

- **Opening balance** — what was owed at the start of the period
- **Line-by-line activity** — every bill, payment, credit note, debit note with running balance
- **Closing balance** — what's owed at the end
- **Matched status** — which items have been settled and which are still open
- **Aged breakdown** — open items bucketed by days past due

For suppliers, use control account **2111**. For customers, use **1141**.

[Open Partner Statement](/dashboard/accounting/partner-statement)

## Trial Balance

Verifies that total debits equal total credits across all accounts. If they don't match, something needs investigation.

[Open Trial Balance](/dashboard/accounting/reports/trial-balance)

## Balance Sheet

Your company's financial position at a point in time:
- **Assets** — Cash, Bank, Receivables, Inventory
- **Liabilities** — Payables, Accrued Expenses
- **Equity** — Capital, Retained Earnings

[Open Balance Sheet](/dashboard/accounting/reports/balance-sheet)

## Income Statement (Profit & Loss)

Revenue minus expenses for a period. Shows sales, cost of goods, operating expenses, and net income.

[Open Income Statement](/dashboard/accounting/reports/income-statement)

## General Ledger
