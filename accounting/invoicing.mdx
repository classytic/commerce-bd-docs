# Invoicing

Create invoices, bills, receipts, and quotes — automatically or by hand.

---

## How invoicing fits in

Invoicing sits between your sales/purchases and the accounting ledger. When you post an invoice, the system automatically creates the journal entry in the ledger. You never need to touch the ledger manually for invoices.

```
Sale / Purchase / POS
      |
   Invoice (draft -> posted)
      |
   Journal Entry (auto-created)
      |
   Reports (aging, trial balance, P&L)
```

---

## Types of documents

| Type | When to use | Number format |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Invoice** | You sell to a customer on credit | INV-2026-00001 |
| **Bill** | You buy from a supplier | BILL-2026-00001 |
| **Credit Note** | Customer returns goods or you give a discount | CN-2026-0001 |
| **Receipt** | POS cash/card sale (no credit) | RCT-2026-0001 |
| **Quote** | Send a price estimate before selling | INV-2026-00001 (after convert) |

## Creating an invoice

1. Go to **Invoices** and click **New Invoice**
2. Select the **customer** and choose a **currency**
3. Add line items — product, quantity, price
4. Optionally set a **due date** or pick a **payment term** (e.g. Net 30)
5. Click **Post** to finalize

> Once posted, the invoice gets a number and a journal entry is created automatically. You cannot edit a posted invoice — use Credit Note to make corrections.

[Open Invoices](/dashboard/accounting/invoices)

## Recording a payment

1. Open the posted invoice
2. Click **Record Payment** in the actions menu
3. Enter the amount, method (cash, bKash, bank transfer), and reference
4. The system updates the balance — partial payments are supported

When fully paid, the status changes to **Paid** and the paid date is recorded.

---

## Credit notes

If a customer returns goods or you need to adjust an invoice:

1. Open the posted invoice
2. Click **Credit Note** in the actions menu
3. Choose **Full** (entire invoice) or **Partial** (specific lines/amounts)
4. The credit note is created as a draft — post it to finalize

Credit notes are linked to the original invoice for audit trail.

---

## Quotes (estimates)

Quotes let you send a price estimate before creating an invoice:

1. Click **New Quote** on the invoices page
2. Fill in customer, lines, expiry date
3. Click **Send** to mark it sent
4. When the customer agrees, click **Accept** then **Convert to Invoice**
5. The quote becomes a draft invoice ready to post
