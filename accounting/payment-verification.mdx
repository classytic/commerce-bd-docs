# Payment Verification

Manual payments (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, bank transfer, cash at counter) don't settle themselves — an admin has to confirm the money actually arrived before the order is treated as paid. This page walks through that review.

---

## What needs verification

Every payment created via a **manual gateway** lands in **Pending** status:

- bKash / Nagad / Rocket (MFS) — customer sends to your merchant number, quotes a trxID
- Bank transfer — customer deposits to your account, attaches a slip
- Cash at counter — counter staff enters the payment, manager signs off

Gateway payments (SSLCommerz, Stripe, card checkout) skip this — the gateway confirms receipt automatically, so those land as **Verified** on creation.

---

## Review queue

Every order with a pending payment shows a yellow **Payment Verification Required** banner on its detail page:

```
⚠ Payment Verification Required
   This payment is pending verification. Review and take action.
   [Reject]  [Verify Payment]
```

Click **Verify Payment** to approve, or **Reject** to decline.

---

## Before you verify

Check the payment details panel. For MFS:

| Check | What to look for |
|---|---|
| **Wallet number** | Matches your merchant number |
| **Transaction reference** | Valid bKash/Nagad trxID format |
| **Amount** | Exactly matches the order total |
| **Timestamp** | Recent (not reused from an old order) |

For bank transfers:

| Check | What to look for |
|---|---|
| **Bank name + account number** | Matches an account you own |
| **Branch** | Sensible for the customer's region |
| **Slip image** | Uploaded, legible, dated |

Your most reliable check is the bKash / Nagad merchant app or bank statement — cross-reference the trxID there before approving.

---

## Verify

1. Confirm the money is in your account
2. Click **Verify Payment**
3. Confirm the dialog

The system marks the transaction `verified`, sets `verifiedAt` and `verifiedBy` (your user), and transitions the order's payment state from `pending` to `paid`. Downstream: the order becomes confirmable, fulfillment can proceed.

> Once verified, the order can move forward — if the customer later disputes, use [Issue Refund](/accounting/order-refunds), not a re-verification reversal.

---

## Reject

Use Reject if:

- The trxID is fake, reused, or doesn't appear in your merchant app
- The amount is short and the customer won't top up
- The deposit slip is illegible or points to an account that isn't yours
- The customer changed their mind before verification and asked to cancel

Enter a **Rejection Reason** (shown on the order and in the audit trail). On submit:

- Transaction status → `failed`
- Order payment state → remains pending (awaiting a new payment attempt) or moves to `failed` depending on the FSM
- The customer is notified (if notifications are configured for this payment method)

> Rejecting is reversible only by the admin role. Double-check before clicking — an incorrectly rejected bKash payment frustrates customers who genuinely paid.

---

## Accounting side

Verification itself does NOT post to the books — money landed in your bank the moment it was transferred, regardless of when you click "verify." What the verification does is **tie that money to a specific order** so A/R for that order clears.

The accounting entries depend on the payment method:

| Method | On verify, posts |
|---|---|
| bKash / Nagad / Rocket | Dr 1122 Mobile Banking, Cr 1141 A/R |
| Bank transfer | Dr 1112 Bank, Cr 1141 A/R |
| Cash at counter | Dr 1111 Cash in Hand, Cr 1141 A/R |

(Only fires if the order was invoiced — POS counter sales post directly without an A/R hop.)

---

## History and audit

Every transaction, verified or not, shows up in the order's **Payment History** tab:

- Amount, method, status, timestamp
- Gateway / manual flag
- Who verified (if verified)
- Rejection reason (if rejected)

Rejected transactions are retained for audit — they aren't deleted. If you reject in error and the customer actually paid, **don't un-reject**; create a fresh payment entry instead.

## Permissions

| Role | Can verify / reject |
|---|---|
| `admin`, `branch_manager` | Yes |
| `cashier` | Cash at counter only |
| `store-staff`, `inventory_staff` | No — view only |
