# Cash on Delivery Settlement

COD orders don't put cash in your bank on day one — the courier collects it, deducts their commission, and remits the net days later. This page explains how the books stay correct from placement to final settlement.

---

## The money's journey

```
Order placed (COD)              Courier delivers               You record settlement
-----------------------------   ------------------------       ---------------------------
Customer owes ৳1,000            Customer pays courier          Bank +৳900, Commission +৳90,
A/R opens on books              ৳1,000 cash                     Writeoff +৳10, A/R clears
```

Three events, three journal entries, one story. You only do the last step manually — the first two are automatic.

---

## At order placement

When a COD order is created, the system posts this entry automatically:

| Account | Dr | Cr |
|---|---|---|
| 1141 Accounts Receivable (linked to this order) | ৳1,000 | |
| 4115 Sales Discount (only if a promo was used) | (promo amt) | |
| 4111 Domestic Sales Revenue | | (gross) |
| 2132 VAT Output Payable (if applicable) | | (VAT) |

**Why A/R and not Cash?** The money isn't yours yet. Posting cash here would overstate your bank balance and hide the collection risk. A/R correctly shows "this order is promised money, not received money."

**Maturity date**: the A/R entry carries an expected remittance date (default 14 days from placement) so aging reports flag couriers who delay payouts.

---

## When the courier pays you

Once the courier remits for a batch of orders, go to the order and click **Record COD Settlement**. Enter:

| Field | What to enter | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Actual received** | What hit your bank / cash | ৳900 |
| **Courier commission** | What the courier deducted | ৳90 |
| **Writeoff** | Unrecovered shortfall (refused, lost, damaged) | ৳10 |
| **Deposit account** | Bank (1112) or Cash in Hand (1111) | Bank |
| **Notes** | Optional reference for your records | Pathao batch 2026-04-23 |

The sum **must equal the original COD amount** (৳1,000 in this example). The system rejects mismatched totals before posting.

On submit, this journal posts:

| Account | Dr | Cr |
|---|---|---|
| 1112 Bank (or 1111 Cash, per your choice) | ৳900 | |
| 6423 Courier COD Commission | ৳90 | |
| 6702 Bad Debt Written Off | ৳10 | |
| 1141 Accounts Receivable (same order) | | ৳1,000 |

A/R now nets to zero for that order. The commission lands in selling & distribution expense; the writeoff lands below operating profit.

## Cancelling a COD order

If a COD order is cancelled **before** settlement (customer returned at doorstep, courier couldn't deliver, etc.), use **Cancel Order**. The system posts a reversal of the placement entry:

| Account | Dr | Cr |
|---|---|---|
| 4111 Domestic Sales Revenue | (gross) | |
| 2132 VAT Output Payable | (VAT) | |
| 4115 Sales Discount reversal | | (promo amt) |
| 1141 Accounts Receivable | | (full) |

A/R clears, revenue reverses, books balance.

> Don't use Cancel after settlement. If money already hit your bank, use **Issue Refund** instead — see [Order Refunds](/accounting/order-refunds).

---

## Partial collection — the three cases

**Courier paid in full, no fee** (rare): actualReceived = gross, commission = 0, writeoff = 0.

**Courier paid net of commission** (normal): actualReceived + commission = gross, writeoff = 0.

**Courier short-paid** (customer partial-refused, package damaged in transit): all three fields have values and sum to gross. Writeoff captures the hole.

## Reporting

Three accounts to watch for COD health:

| Account | What it tells you |
|---|---|
| 1141 A/R (filter by maturityDate overdue) | Couriers who haven't remitted on time |
| 6423 Courier COD Commission | Monthly commission cost — negotiate if it spikes |
| 6702 Bad Debt Written Off | Lost revenue from refused / damaged COD orders |

Run **A/R Aging** with the "COD" filter to see which batches are overdue for settlement.
