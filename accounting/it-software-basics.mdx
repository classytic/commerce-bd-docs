# IT / software basics

You run an IT, software, or ITES company. NBR gives the sector preferential
treatment but the rules split between domestic and export revenue.

## The two revenue streams

### 1. Domestic services → 5% VAT (ITES)

- Reduced rate for ITES (IT-Enabled Services) category.
- Apply tax class `REDUCED_5` on your invoices to domestic BD clients.
- **You cannot claim input VAT on these** — reduced-rate supplies are
  truncated, NBR doesn't allow offsetting.
- Input VAT on your operating costs (office rent, utilities) at standard
  15% can still be claimed against any STANDARD-rate revenue you have.

### 2. Foreign service exports → 0% VAT (zero-rated)

- Buyer country ≠ BD → system automatically remaps to `ZERO_EXPORT`
  via the INTERNATIONAL fiscal position.
- Input VAT on overhead IS claimable (via 1150.VAT0.INPUT → refund).
- You need proof of supply: foreign client invoice, remittance, proof
  of IP/service delivery.

## SEZ / BHTC benefits

If you're based in a Special Economic Zone or Bangabandhu Hi-Tech City,
mark the branch `sezStatus: SEZ` or `BHTC`. SRO-186/2023 gives you:

- 80% VAT rebate on utility bills.
- Streamlined VAT registration.
- Additional income-tax exemptions (handled in your IT return, not here).

When a utility supplier invoices your SEZ/BHTC unit, system applies the
`SEZ_BHTC_UTILITY` fiscal position — rebate is handled as a post-entry
credit line (not a rate change).

## ITES certificate (Finance Act 2024 / SRO-170/2024)

IT/ITES companies can claim a full VAT/CIT exemption until **June 2027**
if they hold a valid BCC (Bangladesh Computer Council) certificate.

- Renew yearly — attach to annual filings.
- Stored in your branch settings (future enhancement).
- When active, the exemption applies automatically via SRO reference.

## What the system does automatically

- **Domestic ITES invoice**: 5% VAT via REDUCED_5 tax class.
- **Foreign export invoice**: INTERNATIONAL fiscal position → 0% VAT.
- **Utility bill to SEZ/BHTC unit**: 80% rebate flagged for post-entry.
- **Monthly Mushak 9.1**: splits output by rate (5% ITES vs 0% export).

## Common questions

**Are domestic + export invoices filed together?**
Yes — one Mushak 9.1 per branch per month, with sections for standard,
reduced, zero-rated, and exempt. The system aggregates per rate for you.

**Call-center services — are those different?**
Call center services have a separate exemption under older SROs. Mark
the product as `EXEMPT_GOVT` or use a custom tax class added via
`extraTaxClasses` in the engine config.
