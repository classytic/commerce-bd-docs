# Send & Track a Quotation

After you've created a draft, here's how to get it in front of the customer and follow up.

---

## Send the quote

1. Open the draft from **Orders > Quotations**
2. Click **Send to customer** (or use the row action menu on the list)
3. Confirm the recipient

The quote moves from **Draft** to **Sent**, and a `sentAt` timestamp is recorded.

### What "send" actually does

The send action **updates the quote's status**. How the customer receives it depends on your setup:

- If your store has email configured, an email goes out automatically with the quote details
- If not, the action is just a status change — you copy the quote link manually (WhatsApp, in-person printout, etc.)

Either way, the quote status reflects that you've shared it. The customer-facing view becomes accessible.

[Go to Quotations](/dashboard/quotations)

---

## Track viewed status

A quote moves from **Sent** to **Viewed** when:

- The customer opens the email link (if email tracking is on), OR
- A staff member manually marks it as viewed (use the action menu — useful for in-person/phone quotes)

The `viewedAt` timestamp tells you the exact moment. This is useful for follow-up — if a quote has been viewed but not accepted in 24-48 hours, that's a good prompt to call.

You don't have to wait for "viewed" to accept — you can skip straight from sent to accepted.

---

## Mark as accepted

When the customer agrees:

1. Open the quote
2. Click **Mark accepted**
3. Confirm

Status moves to **Accepted**, `acceptedAt` is recorded.

The quote is now ready to convert into a real order. See [Convert a Quote to an Order](/dashboard/orders/learn/convert-to-order).

### Approval chains (optional)

Some businesses need an internal sign-off before a quote can be accepted (e.g., the sales manager approves discounts > 20%). When approvals are configured, the **Mark accepted** button is gated:

- All required approvers must approve first
- The approvals panel on the quote shows who has approved/skipped/is pending
- If approval is required and not granted, the accept attempt fails with a clear error message

Approval setup lives in **Settings > Approval Workflows** (admin-only).

---

## Mark as rejected

If the customer declines:

1. Open the quote
2. Click **Reject**
3. Enter a reason (price, timing, competitor, etc.)

Status moves to **Rejected**, `rejectedAt` is recorded, and the reason is saved.

**Rejected is terminal.** You can't un-reject. If the customer changes their mind:

- Clone the quote (creates a new draft with the same lines)
- Adjust price or terms based on what changed
- Send the new quote

This keeps a clean audit trail — both the original rejection and the new attempt are visible.

## Common follow-up patterns

| Status | Suggested action |
|---|---|
| Sent — 24h | Confirm delivery (email landed? customer opened it?) |
| Viewed — 48h, no response | Phone call follow-up |
| Viewed — 7 days, no response | Soft nudge: "Anything blocking acceptance?" |
| Accepted — same day | Convert to order, start fulfillment |
| Expired | Reach out: "Still interested? We can refresh the quote." |

Use the search and filter on the quotations list to find quotes by status and age. Sort by **last updated** to see what's been sitting.
