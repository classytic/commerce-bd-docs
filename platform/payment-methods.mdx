# Payment Methods

Tell the system which payment methods you accept. The POS and online checkout only show options you've enabled here.

---

## Where to Configure

Go to **Settings > Platform Config** and switch to the **Payment Methods** tab. Click **Add Payment Method** to start.

[Open Platform Config](/dashboard/platform-config)

---

## Cash

Cash on delivery, in-store cash, petty cash drawer.

- **Type**: `cash`
- **Name**: e.g. "Cash" or "Cash on Delivery"
- **Active**: on

That's it. No additional fields required.

---

## Mobile Financial Services (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay)

For each MFS account you accept:

- **Type**: `mfs`
- **Provider**: bkash / nagad / rocket / upay
- **Name**: e.g. "bKash Personal — Gulshan"
- **Wallet Number**: the merchant's wallet number (e.g. `01700000000`)
- **Wallet Name**: registered wallet name
- **Active**: on

You can add **multiple MFS entries** — e.g. one bKash personal + one Nagad merchant. Each shows up as a separate option at checkout.

> **Validation:** MFS entries require both `provider` and `walletNumber`. The form won't save without them.

---

## Bank Transfer

For each bank account you accept transfers to:

- **Type**: `bank_transfer`
- **Name**: e.g. "DBBL Current"
- **Bank Name**: full bank name (e.g. "Dutch Bangla Bank")
- **Account Number**, **Account Name**, **Branch Name**, **Routing Number**: from your bank statement

The customer sees these details on the invoice so they know where to wire the money.

---

## Card Payments

For card processors (visa, mastercard, amex, etc.):

- **Type**: `card`
- **Name**: e.g. "City Bank Cards"
- **Card Types**: pick at least one (visa, mastercard, amex, unionpay, other)
- **Bank Name**: optional, the acquiring bank

> **Validation:** Card entries require at least one card type — leave the array empty and the form rejects the save.

---

## Reorder, Disable, or Remove

In the Payment Methods table:

- **Drag** rows to reorder — the order here is the order shown to customers
- Toggle **Active** off to hide a method without deleting it (useful when an account is temporarily out of service)
- Click **Delete** to remove permanently — only do this for methods that have **never been used** on an invoice
