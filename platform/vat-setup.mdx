# VAT & BIN Setup

Required if your business is registered with Bangladesh NBR. Configures VAT rates, BIN, and Mushak invoice numbering.

---

## Mark Yourself as VAT-Registered

Go to **Settings > Platform Config > VAT** and toggle **VAT Registered** on.

If you're NOT VAT-registered, leave this off — invoices will skip the VAT line and the Mushak fields, and you can run on simplified accounting.

[Open Platform Config](/dashboard/platform-config)

---

## Enter Your BIN

The **Business Identification Number (BIN)** is the 13-digit ID from NBR. Format: `123456789-0101`.

Enter it in the **BIN** field. The system validates the format — wrong shape gets rejected.

Your BIN appears on every Mushak 6.3 invoice and is required for VAT returns.

---

## Set the Default VAT Rate

Most businesses use **15%** (the standard VAT rate). Some product categories qualify for reduced rates (5%, 7.5%, 10%) — set these per-product, not at the platform level.

The default rate applies to any product that doesn't have its own override.

---

## Prices Include VAT?

Two ways to display prices:

- **Inclusive** (recommended for retail) — the price you set IS the customer pays. The system reverse-calculates the VAT for the invoice.
- **Exclusive** (recommended for B2B) — the price you set is the base. VAT is added on top at checkout.

Toggle **Prices Include VAT** based on your business model. Switching this **does not change** existing prices — only how new invoices break down the totals.

---

## Mushak Invoice Numbering

Mushak 6.3 invoices need a sequential number with a configurable prefix.

Set:

- **Invoice Prefix**: e.g. `INV-` (appears before the year + sequence)
- **Start Number**: usually `1` for new businesses, or your last NBR-filed number + 1 if you're migrating mid-year

Generated format: `INV-2026-000001`. The system increments atomically — no duplicates even under load.

---

## Mushak 9.1 (Returns)

Mushak filings happen monthly. The system can auto-generate a return draft on the 1st of each month — toggle **Auto-Generate Returns** in the same VAT panel.

Generated returns appear in **Accounting > Mushak Returns** for review before submission.

[View Mushak Returns](/dashboard/accounting/musok)
