# Getting Started

Loyalty rewards customers for repeat business. Set this up once and members earn points on every order; spend them later for discounts.

---

## How it works (in 30 seconds)

```
Customer enrolls    →   Member with card ID + balance
Customer buys       →   Earning rule fires    →   Points added
Customer reaches X  →   Tier auto-upgrades    →   Better multipliers + benefits
Customer redeems    →   Points reserved → confirmed → discount applied
Customer refers     →   Referee signs up      →   Both earn bonus points
```

The system tracks an immutable **point ledger** behind the scenes — every earn, redeem, adjust, and expire is auditable.

---

## 1. Define Your Tiers

Tiers are the membership levels — Silver, Gold, Platinum, etc. Each tier has its own earning multiplier and benefits.

Go to **Loyalty > Tiers** and click **New Tier**. For each tier:

- **Name + Rank** — display name and ordering (1 = lowest)
- **Qualification** — minimum lifetime points OR minimum lifetime spend (per period, e.g. 90 days)
- **Benefits** — earning multiplier (e.g. 1.5×), discount %, free shipping, custom flags
- **Downgrade grace** — days at lower spend before the member drops a tier (default: 30)

Tiers re-evaluate automatically when members earn or redeem. Use **Evaluate All** if you change qualification rules and want to recompute everyone immediately.

[Open Tiers](/dashboard/loyalty/tiers)

## 2. Add Earning Rules

Rules decide how many points a customer earns for each action.

Go to **Loyalty > Earning Rules** and click **New Rule**. Pick a type:

- **Order** — points based on order amount (e.g. "1 point per BDT 10 spent")
- **Action** — fixed points for events like signup, birthday, first order, write a review
- **Category** — bonus points when buying from a specific product category
- **Tier Bonus** — multiplier applied to a specific tier (e.g. "Gold members earn 2× on weekends")

Each rule has:

- **Conditions** — minimum order amount, eligible categories/tiers, day-of-week, date range
- **Reward** — fixed points OR points-per-unit OR multiplier
- **Caps** — max points per transaction (prevents one whale order from breaking the budget)
- **Priority** — when multiple rules match, lower priority numbers win

Activate rules with **Activate**; pause with **Pause**.

[Open Earning Rules](/dashboard/loyalty/earning-rules)

---

## 3. Enroll Customers

Customers become members in two ways:

- **Self-enroll** — they hit "Join" on the storefront or in the customer portal (calls `POST /loyalty/me/enroll`)
- **Admin enroll** — go to **Customers > select a customer > Loyalty tab > Enroll**

Each member gets:
- A **card ID** (e.g. `MBR-DHK-00004821-8`) for POS scanning
- A **referral code** (8 chars, distinct from card ID) to share with friends
- Balance: `current` (spendable) + `lifetime` (all-time earned) + `redeemed` + `expired`

[Open Customers](/dashboard/customers)

---

## 4. Test the Flow

1. Enroll a test customer
2. Place an order at the POS or storefront for them
3. Open **Customers > [test customer] > Loyalty tab** and confirm points were earned per your rule
4. Apply their card ID at checkout and redeem points — confirm the discount lands

If points didn't earn: check the rule's status (`active`?), conditions (min amount, category match?), and the customer's tier — they may need to be enrolled or active.
