# Members

Every loyalty participant is a **member** with their own card ID, referral code, balance, and tier.

---

## Enroll a Customer

Go to **Customers > [customer name] > Loyalty tab** and click **Enroll**. The system generates:

- **Card ID** — formatted like `MBR-DHK-00004821-8` (branch code + sequence + check digit). This is what the POS scans at checkout.
- **Referral code** — 8 random characters (e.g. `K7P2-N9X4`). Different from the card ID — used for referrals only.

The customer immediately has a balance of `0` and is in the lowest active tier.

[Open Customers](/dashboard/customers)

> **Self-enrollment** also works — customers click **Join Loyalty** on the storefront. The dashboard view is the admin-side fallback.

---

## View Member Details

The Loyalty tab on a customer profile shows:

| Field | What it means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Card ID** | The barcode/string for POS scanning |
| **Status** | `active`, `inactive`, or `suspended` |
| **Tier** | Current tier name and rank |
| **Tier expiry** | When this tier review happens; auto-extends as long as qualification holds |
| **Current balance** | Spendable points |
| **Lifetime earned** | All points ever earned (never decreases) |
| **Redeemed** | Total points spent on discounts |
| **Expired** | Points that aged out before being used |

The **History** panel below shows a paginated transaction ledger — every earn, redeem, adjust, expire, transfer, and referral bonus, in reverse chronological order.

## Manually Adjust Points

For one-off corrections (e.g. customer service goodwill, bug compensation):

1. Open the member's Loyalty tab
2. Click **Adjust Points**
3. Enter a positive number to add, negative to subtract
4. Add a **reason** (required — appears in the audit trail)
5. Save

The adjustment shows up as a `adjust` transaction with your user ID logged. There's no undo — to reverse a mistake, do another adjustment with the opposite sign and a corrective reason.

---

## Suspend or Reactivate

If a member abuses the program (e.g. fraudulent referrals, bot signups):

1. Open the member's Loyalty tab
2. Click **Deactivate** or **Suspend**

A `suspended` member:
- **Cannot earn** new points (rules skip them)
- **Cannot redeem** existing balance
- Keeps their card ID, balance, history (so you can investigate or restore)

Click **Reactivate** to restore full member status. The balance is intact — nothing is forfeited automatically.

---

## Tier Override (Admin)

For VIPs or promotional boosts, you can manually lock a member into a tier regardless of their qualification:

1. Open the member's Loyalty tab
2. Click **Set Tier Override**
3. Pick a tier and write a reason (e.g. "Loyal partner since 2018, manual lock")
4. Save

While the override is in place:
- Auto-tier evaluation is skipped for this member
- Only an admin can clear it (click **Clear Override**)
- The override + reason appear in the audit trail

[Open Tiers](/dashboard/loyalty/tiers)

## Search by Card ID

The POS scans a member's card ID and looks them up via `GET /loyalty/members/by-card/:cardId`. From the dashboard, just type the card ID into the **Customers** search bar — the matching member surfaces with their loyalty data attached.
