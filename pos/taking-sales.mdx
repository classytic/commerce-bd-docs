# Taking Sales

This is what the cashier does 95% of the time. Learn this flow cold.

---

## The Five-Step Sale

```
1. Find product   →  search, scan, or pick from category
2. Add to cart    →  click product card (auto-adds simple, prompts variant if needed)
3. Adjust         →  change quantity, apply discount, look up customer
4. Checkout       →  pick payment method, enter amount, complete
5. Receipt        →  print or email
```

Press **F2** after the receipt to start a new sale.

---

## Finding Products

Three ways:

**Scan** — point the barcode scanner at the item. The system auto-finds the product (and the right variant if the barcode is variant-specific) and adds it to the cart. Fastest path.

**Search** — type any of: product name, SKU, partial name, partial SKU. The catalog matches in real time. Use for items without barcodes.

**Browse** — pick a category from the left panel, then scroll the product grid. Use for new staff still learning the catalog or for browsing-style stores.

The catalog is **branch-aware** — only products with stock at the active branch appear (you can toggle "Show all" if you need to look up an out-of-stock item, e.g. for a transfer request).

---

## Variants

When a product has variants (size, color, etc.), tapping it opens the **variant picker**:

- Pick the size
- Pick the color
- Confirm — the specific variant goes to the cart

Variants with zero stock at this branch appear greyed out unless "Show out of stock" is on. The cashier can still pick them — useful for special orders that get fulfilled later.

---

## The Cart

The cart shows everything the customer is buying. For each line:

- **Quantity** — `+` and `-` buttons to adjust, or click the number to type
- **Unit price** — read-only (changing prices requires manager auth)
- **Line total** — qty × unit price
- **Remove** — trash icon

Top of the cart:
- **Customer** field — search by name/phone or scan a loyalty card
- **Voucher / promo code** field
- **Discount** button — opens manager-auth dialog for arbitrary discounts

Bottom of the cart:
- Subtotal
- Discounts (auto-applied promotions, manual discounts, loyalty redemptions)
- VAT (if applicable)
- **Total** — what the customer pays

---

## Applying a Discount

Three discount paths:

**Auto-promotion** — if a [promotion program](/commerce-bd/docs/promotions/programs) matches the cart (e.g. "Eid 10% off"), the discount applies automatically. The cart shows it as a separate line with the program name.

**Voucher code** — type or scan a code into the **Promo** field, hit Enter. The system validates the code (active? within expiry? remaining uses?) and applies the discount, OR shows an error.

**Manual discount** — for one-off goodwill or negotiated discounts. Click **Discount**, enter a manager PIN if the amount exceeds the cashier's limit, type the discount amount or percentage, save. This is logged with the manager's user ID.

Discounts **stack only if the program allows it** — see [Promotions stacking modes](/commerce-bd/docs/promotions/programs).

## Loyalty (If Customer Is a Member)

If you've selected a customer who has loyalty:

1. Their **current balance** appears in the cart
2. The system shows **max redeemable** for this cart (capped at e.g. 50% of subtotal — see [Redemption](/commerce-bd/docs/loyalty/redemption))
3. Click **Redeem** → enter how many points to use → confirm
4. The discount lands on the cart, points are **reserved** (not yet consumed)

If the customer changes their mind before paying, just remove the redemption — points return to balance immediately.

After payment, points are **confirmed** (officially redeemed) and the customer also **earns** new points based on the order amount per your [earning rules](/commerce-bd/docs/loyalty/earning-rules).

## Checkout — Payment

Click **Checkout** to leave the cart and enter payment.

Payment screen shows:
- **Total due**
- **Payment methods** (cash, bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Card, etc.) — only the methods you've enabled in [Platform Config](/commerce-bd/docs/platform/payment-methods) appear

Pick a method:

**Cash** — enter the amount tendered. The screen shows **change** = tendered − total. Hand over change.

**Mobile money (bKash/Nagad/Rocket)** — enter the wallet TXN ID confirming the customer's payment. The system stores the reference for reconciliation.

**Card** — enter the bank slip number / approval code from your card terminal.

### Split payments

Customer wants to pay BDT 5,000 cash + BDT 3,000 bKash? Use **Split**:

1. Click **Split**
2. Add a payment line for cash, amount 5000
3. Add another line for bKash, amount 3000 + TXN ID
4. Confirm — both are stored on the order

The order has a **payment array** with each line. Total of all lines must equal the order total.

---

## Confirming the Sale

After payment, the system:

1. Creates a **real order** (decrements stock, applies promotions, fires events)
2. **Confirms** any reserved loyalty redemption
3. Triggers earning rules → adds new points to the customer's balance
4. Posts to the GL (revenue, VAT, COGS, cash) — happens asynchronously
5. Shows the **Receipt screen**

The receipt has: invoice number, date/time, items + qty + price, discounts, VAT, total, payment breakdown, customer name (if any), loyalty earnings/balance.

Click **Print** for a paper copy, **Email** to send digital, or **Skip** to move on.

Press **F2** to start the next sale.

---

## Refunds

To refund a previous sale:

1. **F9** — open the Order History drawer
2. Find the order (search by date, customer name, invoice number)
3. Click the order → **Refund**
4. Pick:
   - **Full refund** — refunds everything (all items, all discounts, all loyalty)
   - **Partial refund** — pick which items + quantities
5. Pick the **refund method** (back to original payment method, or convert to store credit)
6. Confirm

The system:
- Posts a **refund order** linked to the original
- **Restocks** the returned items
- **Reverses** the loyalty earnings (and credits back any redeemed points)
- Posts to GL (negative revenue, COGS reversal, cash out)

The original order is now marked `partially-refunded` or `fully-refunded`.
